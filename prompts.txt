Here is the Brand Identity: (use only what's needed, use it as guidelines, not strict rules)
Clean, confident, and modern. The brand leans into bold red (#e60f0f) as the sole accent color — used sparingly but decisively on CTAs, tags, and the logo. Everything else is near-white backgrounds with dark neutral text, creating high contrast without visual noise.
Typography
Headers are heavy, extra-bold, large — assertive and dominant, black on white
Body/subtitles are light gray, small, airy — acts as a whisper under the shout of the heading
Navigation links are medium weight, neutral, minimal — no decoration, no fuss
Font feel: modern sans-serif, geometric, professional
Color Palette
Role	Value
Primary accent	#e60f0f
Background	#ffffff / #f7f7f7
Primary text	#111111
Secondary text	#888888
Card background	#ffffff
Borders/dividers	very subtle #e5e5e5
Layout & Spacing
Generous whitespace everywhere — breathes heavily, nothing feels cramped
Top navbar: logo left, nav center, user avatar right — standard but well-balanced
Breadcrumb trail below nav — small, muted, shows hierarchy without taking space
Hero section: massive bold headline, one-liner subtitle, single bold red CTA button — nothing else
Card grid: 4 columns, equal-width, clean card shadow, photo-first with a price/tag overlay badge in top corners
Cards have rounded corners (medium radius ~8–12px), subtle drop shadow
Interactive Elements
Primary buttons: red fill #e60f0f, white text, rounded pill shape, bold label — high energy
Secondary/filter buttons: white or light gray fill, dark text, outlined or soft — lower hierarchy
Tags/badges: small pill badges overlaid on images, red or white fill with tight padding
Links: red text, no underline — consistent with the accent system
Vibe in One Sentence
Bold-headline minimalism with a confident red accent system — lots of air, strict hierarchy, and one color that does all the talking.
